Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

Description

A simple meat based tomato sauce to go with your favorite pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 Jar of your choice of Spaghetti Sauce
  • 16 oz of pasta of your choice
  • 1 lb of Ground Beef (I like 80%/20%)
  • 1/2 c of Italian Bread Crumbs
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water and put on the stove to boil. Add a pinch of salt to season the water.
  2. While the water is boiling, add the Ground Beef to a skillet over medium-high heat and cook until browned.
  3. Once cooked, drain off most of the excess grease with a paper towel. Add the Bread Crumbs to the meat to soak up the rest.
  4. Add your preferred Spaghetti Sauce, mix to combine, and turn the stove down to Low.
  5. Once the pot of water has started to boil, add the pasta. Cook to your desired level of done per the manufacturer's instructions.
  6. When the pasta has about 2-3 minutes left, turn the Spaghetti Sauce up to Medium.
  7. When the pasta is finished, drain the excess water and serve onto plates.
  8. Top with the Spaghetti Sauce and grated Parmesan Cheese.
