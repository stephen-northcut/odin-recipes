Enchilada Lasagna

Description

Enchilada lasagna is a layered cassarole dish. It combines sour cream, chicken, black beans, cheese, and corn tortillas into an easy to make, delicious meal for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Black Beans
  • 1.5 lb of Chicken Breast
  • 14 oz of Sour Cream (the sqeeze packet works perfectly)
  • 1 can of Cream of Celery Soup
  • 1 can of Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 5 cups of Shredded Mexican Cheese
  • At least 15 Corn Tortillas

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 425 Degrees F.
  2. Cube the chicken breast and season with salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and chili powder.
  3. Cook the chicken in a skillet over medium-high heat until cooked through (at least 165 degrees).
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the Black Beans (rinsed), Sour Cream, Cream of Celery Soup, and Cream of Chicken Soup.
  5. In a 13x9 baking pan add a layer of the Sour Cream mixture. Just enough to cloat the bottom of the pan.
  6. Dip Corn Tortillas in water (just long enough to wet them) and lay them in the bottom of the baking pan to make a single layer.
  7. Continue layering in the following order until you have done so twice:
    1. Sour Cream Mixture
    2. Chicken (half of what you cooked)
    3. Shredded Cheese
    4. Wetted Corn Tortillas
  8. Add one more layer of Sour Cream Mixture, then a final layer of cheese.
  9. Place in the oven and cook for 20 minutes or until the cheese on top is just starting to brown.
  10. Remove from the oven and serve.
