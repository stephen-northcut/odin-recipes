Cinnamon Toast

Description

A delicious snack that's quick and easy to prepare. Just combine butter, cinnamon, and sugar on bread and toast it.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • Salted Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Butter one side of the slice of bread.
  2. Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Place into a toaster oven and set to Toast Level 1.
  4. Serve and Enjoy.
